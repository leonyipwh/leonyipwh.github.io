## Overview

Hotel Jen customer experience journey begins right as people enter the lobby. Information, promotions, and recommendations will be displayed on a large digital media wall for guests to learn more about Hotel Jen and the city around them. Animated and visually playful our digital wall is not only informative but also brings to life Jen’s approachable, friendly and funky personality.
​<br />
​At any screen guests can simply use their smartphone to get more information about various events, destinations, restaurants  and hotel facilities.
