Overview

With a sprawling network of underwater cables, carrying a large percentage of Asia Pacific’s web traffic, Telstra Global provide the technology backbone that helps the world connect. Our interactive globe and map allows users to peel back the layers, revealing the infrastructure that brings the internet to the world. Featuring detailed engineering data, and cable performance, the interactive map will be used in a range of scenario’s to showcase Telstra’s infrastructure reach and capabilities.
